# The Ummah AI Initiative — One-Page Brief

> *"وَأَعِدُّوا لَهُم مَّا اسْتَطَعْتُم مِّن قُوَّةٍ"* — Quran 8:60
>
> *Open AI infrastructure for the Muslim Ummah.*

---

## What

An open-source initiative building four projects for the global Muslim community:

- **Basira** (`بَصِيرَة`) — Citation-grounded Islamic research copilot covering Quran, hadith, tafseer, and fiqh across Sunni, Shia, and Ibadi traditions.
- **Shahid** (`شَاهِد`) — Permanent, cryptographically-verified archive of AI-driven harms against civilians.
- **Hisn** (`حِصْن`) — Counter-surveillance toolkit for Muslim activists, journalists, scholars, and at-risk users.
- **Mizan** (`مِيزَان`) — Independent audit platform documenting how major tech platforms treat Muslim content.

## Why

1.8 billion Muslims rely on AI tools that hallucinate hadiths, mistranslate Quran, suppress Palestinian content, and target civilians via algorithmic systems. None of this infrastructure is Muslim-built. The gap is structural and worsens annually.

## Structure (the unusual parts)

- **Non-sectarian:** every Muslim school of thought represented; no school privileged.
- **Founder lifetime return is capped** at a small documented figure ($500K USD indexed). Above the cap, all value flows to mission work.
- **Entity is not for sale.** Categorical clause in founding documents.
- **Decisions made by a 12-member Council** spanning all major Muslim traditions, operating pseudonymously to resist blackmail / honey traps / state pressure.
- **Funding refused** if it demands committee disclosure, editorial control, or governance influence.
- **Open-source.** All standards, tools, and corpora released under MIT / CC BY-SA. Contributors retain full rights to their work.

## What we're asking from you

Different per role; we'll ask you specifically. In summary:

- **Scholars:** verify content; serve eventually on Council; paid retainer.
- **Engineers / researchers:** review architecture; contract for specific epics; possible full-time roles.
- **Partners:** MoUs for shared work (HR orgs, journalism, academic).
- **Funders:** mission-aligned grants or waqfs without strings.

**First commitment is small** (a single review, a brief call, reading the relevant section). No one is asked to commit major time before mutual fit is clear.

## What we will not do

- Take funding that compromises decisions.
- Issue fatawa or substitute for qualified scholarly judgment.
- Build technology that targets civilians.
- Sell, merge, or exit the entity.
- Disclose Council member identities under pressure.
